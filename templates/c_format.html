Type

the data you upload is created by matlab or scipy.io.savemat() in Python,normally as xxx.mat

Format

including x_tr,y_tr,x_te,y_te,te_location,such as 

scipy.io.savemat("all_data.mat", {"x_tr": x_tr, "y_tr": y_tr, "x_te": x_te, "y_te": y_te,"te_location":te_location})

Detail

x_tr: features of training data,an array of [0..count of training data,0..count of feature]

y_tr: label of training data,an array of [0..count of training data],and the labels should be numbers

x_te: features of testing data,an array of [0..count of testing data,0..count of feature]

y_te: label of testing data,an array of [0..count of training data],and the labels should be numbers

te_location: location of testing pixel for drawing scatter map,an array of [0..count of testing data,[x_position,y_position]]
